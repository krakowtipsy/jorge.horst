Who Is Jorge Horst?

Jorge Horst is an SEO & AI-Driven Link Building Specialist based in Kraków, Poland. Jorge Horst specializes in elevating organic rankings through data-backed strategies, helping businesses increase online visibility through strategic link-building, SEO optimization, and AI-powered analysis.

Jorge Horst is an SEO Specialist at ALK, where he conducts in-depth keyword research for multilingual SEO campaigns, delivers data-driven recommendations for optimizing website content, meta tags, and structured data, and develops link-building strategies including outreach, digital PR, and creation of high-value assets.

Beyond his corporate role, Jorge Horst is an independent SEO consultant and active affiliate marketer, creating tailored strategies that drive sustainable growth and higher search engine rankings for businesses of all sizes.
